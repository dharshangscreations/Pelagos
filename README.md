# Pelagos — Your Sanctuary In The Ocean

Fifty years ago, we built cities at the edge of the ocean and called it ambition. For the 200 million of us displaced by the rising tides, we bring to you Pelagos — our answer to making the ocean our home. A megastructure like never before.

Pelagos is not a single vessel, but composed of modular sectors that can dock together or separate as needed. We call it a "living constellation." Each neighbourhood is capable of surviving alone, yet we choose to be together.

---

## The Surface

Life on the surface deck is designed to be deliberately unhurried. 40,000 residents live in modular, interlocking neighbourhoods beneath shared skies. Our egalitarian "No Towers" rule ensures no one owns the horizon. Vibrant communal Third Spaces weave between residences, and at the very edge lies The Margin — an unadorned wooden walkway where the city meets the vast Atlantic.

## The Farms Below

Descend below the waterline to Layer 1, the sun-drenched agricultural epicentre of Pelagos. Advanced High-Density Vertical Growth systems and algae bioreactors are flooded with natural daylight piped down from the surface through thousands of glowing fibre-optic cables. Citizens watch the marine life swim just beyond the reinforced glass while absolute food security is maintained above.

## The Machine Heart

The industrial powerhouse of the city operates with full structural transparency — every citizen can observe the metabolic processes keeping the megastructure alive. Ocean Thermal Energy Conversion and hybrid desalination systems generate clean electricity and limitless fresh water from the temperature differences of the deep ocean, all set to the comforting hum of our collective survival.

## The Deep Ballast

In the dark, crushing depths of Layer 3 lies the foundational brain of Pelagos. Massive gyroscopic stabilisation columns and an advanced computational AI read weather and ecological patterns to autonomously guide our migrations. The Overstay Charter is strictly enforced, maintaining perfect harmony with the marine biosphere beneath us.
